Common Offer Types Explained (Briefly)

  • Deposit Bonuses: The casino matches a percentage of your deposit with bonus funds (e.g., 100% match up to $500).
  • No-Deposit Bonuses: Free bonus money or spins just for signing up. Usually smaller amounts.
  • Free Spins: Awarded on specific slot games. Winnings often have playthrough requirements.
  • Risk-Free Bets/Play: Get a refund (often as bonus money) if your initial bets up to a certain amount lose.

Each offer comes with playthrough (wagering) requirements – the amount you must bet before bonus funds or winnings can be withdrawn. Low playthrough on high Return-to-Player (RTP) games is ideal.
