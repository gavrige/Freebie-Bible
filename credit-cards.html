Top Credit Card Signup Bonuses & Rewards - May 2024

Strategic credit card churning can yield significant rewards, often in the form of cash back or travel points. This involves applying for new credit cards to earn valuable sign-up bonuses.

Important Considerations!

  • Good Credit Required: Most rewards cards require good to excellent credit.
  • Responsible Spending: Never spend more than you can afford just to meet a minimum spend requirement. Pay your balances in full each month to avoid interest charges that negate rewards.
  • Credit Score Impact: Applying for new credit can temporarily lower your credit score. Understand the "5/24 rule" for Chase cards and similar restrictions from other issuers.

Key Tips for Credit Card Bonuses

  • Know Your Credit Score: Ensure it's good enough before applying to avoid denials.
  • Understand Issuer Rules: Be aware of rules like Chase's 5/24, Amex's once-per-lifetime language, etc.
  • Meet Spending Requirements Naturally: Plan applications around large, necessary purchases. Avoid manufactured spending unless you fully understand the risks.
  • Track Everything: Use a spreadsheet for application dates, minimum spend deadlines, annual fee dates, and bonus postings.
  • Consider Annual Fees: Evaluate if the card's benefits outweigh the annual fee after the first year. Downgrade or cancel if not.
