Best Get-Paid-To (GPT) Sites for Extra Cash

What are Get-Paid-To (GPT) Sites?

Get-Paid-To (GPT) sites are platforms that reward users for completing various online tasks. These can include taking surveys, watching videos, playing games, signing up for offers, shopping online, and more. While individual tasks might pay small amounts, consistent effort or focusing on high-value offers can lead to decent earnings.

GPT sites can provide a steady stream of smaller earnings or lead to larger payouts via high-value offers, especially those related to financial products or service trials.

Best Strategy for GPT Sites

While you can earn from surveys and small tasks, the most lucrative approach on GPT sites often involves:

  • High-Paying Offers: Focus on offers for signing up to bank accounts, brokerage apps, casino/sportsbook apps, or subscription trials. These often pay significantly more than standard tasks.
  • Stacking Opportunities: Combine GPT site offers with the actual bonuses from the services themselves (e.g., get paid by Swagbucks for opening a SoFi account, AND get the SoFi new account bonus). See our Bank Bonuses, Online Casinos, and Sports Betting pages for such offers.
  • Game Offers: Many sites pay well for reaching certain levels in mobile games. This can be time-consuming but profitable if you enjoy gaming.
  • Patience and Tracking: Some offers take time to credit. Keep records of what you've completed and when payment is due.

A Word of Caution

Always read the terms of each offer carefully on GPT sites. Ensure you understand the requirements for completion and payment. Some offers may require a purchase or deposit. Only participate in offers you are comfortable with and that make financial sense for you.
