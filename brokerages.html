General Strategy & Order

It's often best to start with offers that have lower deposit requirements and higher relative value (like the Schwab Starter Kit). Then move to offers with slightly higher requirements or those that give random stock values. Always check if a broker performs a hard or soft pull on your credit. (More detailed strategy for Phase 2).

Key Tips for Brokerage Bonuses

  • Understand Bonus Types: Some offer cash, others offer stock (which may have fluctuating value).
  • Deposit Requirements: Ensure you can meet any minimum deposit amounts.
  • Holding Periods: Be aware of how long you need to keep the deposit or bonus in the account before withdrawal.
  • Tax Implications: Brokerage bonuses (cash or fair market value of stock) are generally considered taxable income.
  • Safety of Funds: Stick to reputable, SIPC-insured brokerages.
