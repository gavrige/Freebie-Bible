Earn Money with Paid Focus Groups

What are Paid Focus Groups & Research Studies?

Paid focus groups and research studies are a way for companies and researchers to gather opinions and feedback on products, services, concepts, or advertisements. Participants are compensated for their time and input. These can be conducted online (most common now) or in person.

Focus groups can be a great way to earn a good hourly rate for your opinions, often paying $50-$150 for 30-90 minutes of your time. Some specialized studies can pay even more.

Tips for Qualifying and Participating

  • Complete Your Profile Thoroughly: Most platforms use profile information (demographics, interests, profession) to match you with relevant studies. Keep it updated.
  • Be Honest in Screeners: Answer screening questionnaires truthfully. Trying to guess what researchers want can lead to disqualification or poor quality participation.
  • Check Regularly: New studies are posted frequently. Set up email notifications if available.
  • Be Professional and Articulate: If selected, arrive on time (for online studies, log in early), ensure your tech (webcam, microphone) works, and provide thoughtful, honest feedback.
  • Don't Be Discouraged by Disqualifications: You won't qualify for every study. It's a numbers game. Keep applying.

Focus groups are a legitimate way to earn extra money, but always be cautious of scams. Never pay to participate in a focus group. Reputable companies will pay you for your time.
