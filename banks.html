Key Tips for Bank Bonuses

  • Check for Fees: Always be aware of monthly maintenance fees and how to waive them (e.g., minimum balance, direct deposits).
  • Direct Deposit Methods: Employer payroll is standard. Some banks accept ACH transfers from other banks or services like PayPal/Venmo. Using an EIN for side hustle income to pay yourself via payroll services can also work.
  • Track Your Accounts: Keep a spreadsheet of account opening dates, bonus requirements, and when you can close accounts without penalty (usually 6 months).
  • Use Aggregators: Sites like Doctor of Credit are excellent resources for finding current offers and data points.
  • Read the Fine Print: Eligibility (new customer definitions), qualifying actions, and payout timelines are crucial.
